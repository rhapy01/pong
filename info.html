SIGN PONG

How to Play

Sign Pong is a modern take on the classic Pong game. The objective is simple: use your paddle to hit the ball past your opponent's paddle to score points. The first player to reach 10 points wins the game!

Game Modes

Single Player Mode

Challenge yourself against the computer's advanced AI in a race to 10 points.

  • How to Start: Click the "Single Player" button on the main menu to begin immediately.
  • Difficulty Levels: Adjust the difficulty by clicking the "Difficulty" button on the main menu.
  • Easy Mode: The AI moves slower and has less precise predictions, perfect for beginners.
  • Normal Mode: Balanced AI that provides a moderate challenge for most players.
  • Hard Mode: Advanced AI with faster reactions and more accurate predictions.
  • Expert Mode: Nearly professional-level AI that will test even the most skilled players.
  • Scoring: First player to reach 10 points wins the match.
  • Game Over: When either you or the computer reaches 10 points, a game over screen will appear showing the winner.
  • Share Your Score: After the game ends, you can share your results on social media or copy the link to share with friends.

Online Multiplayer Mode

Challenge other players in real-time matches over the internet.

  • How to Start: Click the "Online Multiplayer" button on the main menu.
  • Player Name: Enter your display name that other players will see.
  • Creating a Room:
    1. Click "Create Room" to generate a unique room code.
    2. Share this code with your friend who wants to join your game.
    3. Wait for your opponent to join - the game will start automatically when they connect.
  • Joining a Room:
    1. Click "Join Room" on the online menu.
    2. Enter the room code provided by your friend.
    3. Click "Join" to connect to their game.
  • Quick Match: Click "Quick Match" to be automatically paired with any available player.
  • Match Format: Online matches consist of two sets with players switching sides between sets.
  • Rest Period: There's a short break between sets to prepare for the side switch.
  • Scoring: The player with the highest total score across both sets wins the match.
  • Disconnections: If your opponent disconnects, you'll be returned to the main menu.
  • Share Your Score: After the match ends, you can share your results on social media platforms or copy the link to share with friends.

Controls

  • W or ↑ - Move paddle up
  • S or ↓ - Move paddle down
  • ESC - Pause game

Game Features

  • Dynamic Ball Speed: The ball gradually speeds up during rallies for increasing challenge.
  • Angle Control: The ball's angle changes based on where it hits your paddle, allowing for strategic shots.
  • Human-like AI: The computer opponent features advanced physics-based movement that mimics human players.
  • Score Tracking: Clear score display shows the current score and indicates who's winning.

Tips

  • Hit the ball with the edges of your paddle to create sharper angles.
  • Try to anticipate where the ball will go rather than just reacting.
  • In higher difficulties, the AI will be more precise and responsive.
  • Practice timing your movements to improve your reaction speed.
